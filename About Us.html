About Special Cakes for All Occasions

Our Story

Special Cakes for All Occasions was founded by Sam Rice with a passion for creating delicious and visually stunning cakes. In just one year, the bakery has become a favourite destination for customers looking for something sweet and special to celebrate life’s big moments. We believe that every cake tells a story — whether it’s a wedding, birthday, or celebration, our mission is to make it memorable through taste, design, and heart.
